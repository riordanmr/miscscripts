St. Maurice Grade School Class of 1970

Welcome, St. Maurice class of 1970!

We are planning a reunion to be held on Aug 19, 2023 at the former St. Maurice Church (now St. Rafka) at 32765 Lyndon St, Livonia, MI 48154. We hope you all can make it. Here is the schedule of events:

  • 4pm: St. Rafka will be holding their annual festival at the church during the reunion, so there will be plenty of food, drink, and entertainment available. Look for the table labeled St. Maurice Reunion. Several of us will be setting up the table half an hour before the festival.
  • Late evening: Meet at Steve Sprietzer's house at 14467 Parklane, Livonia.
